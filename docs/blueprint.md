# **App Name**: Craving

## Core Features:

- Vendor Registration & Profile: Allows street food vendors to sign up, create their stall profile, and manage basic business information.
- Dynamic QR Menu Management: Vendors can create, update, and manage digital menus with dish names, prices, and availability, associated with scannable QR codes.
- Mobile Customer Ordering: Customers can scan a vendor's QR, browse the menu, customize orders, and submit them directly from their mobile device.
- Digital Payment Integration: Enables customers to complete orders using secure cashless transactions via integrated payment gateways.
- AI Dish Description Assistant: A generative AI tool to assist vendors in creating engaging and appetizing descriptions for their menu items, boosting sales.
- Vendor Order Management: Provides vendors with a simple interface to view, accept, and manage incoming customer orders in real-time.
- Customer Loyalty Program: Implements a basic loyalty system to track customer purchases and offer rewards, like a 'Buy X, Get Y Free' incentive.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A vibrant, inviting amber orange (#F2960D) to evoke a sense of delicious food and warmth, balancing tradition with modernity.
- Background color: A soft, warm off-white (#FAF8F5) derived from the primary hue, providing a clean and approachable canvas.
- Accent color: A rich, slightly deeper red-orange (#C14627) as an analogous complement to the primary, for effective calls to action and highlighting.
- All text: 'Inter', a grotesque sans-serif font, chosen for its modern, clean appearance, excellent readability, and versatility across both headlines and body content.
- Utilize clear and consistent stroke-based icons from the Lucide-React library to maintain a clean, modern aesthetic.
- Adopt a responsive, mobile-first, and grid-based layout for optimal display on diverse devices, featuring a distinct hero section and clear feature presentation.
- Incorporate subtle, directional entry animations (e.g., slide-in effects) for key interface elements to enhance user engagement and visual flow without being intrusive.
